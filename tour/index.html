How Check-Ins Work

  • Get reminded - Notification arrives at your scheduled time
  • Multiple ways to check in:
    • Tap the "I'm OK ✓" button on the notification itself (no app open)
    • Tap the Check In button on the home screen widget (iOS 17+, no app open)
    • Open the app and tap Check In
  • Grace period starts - If you don't respond, you get 5-30 minutes with repeating urgent alerts
  • Automatic SMS - After grace period ends, your confirmed contacts receive emergency alerts

What Happens in an Emergency

  • Urgent notifications - Repeating alerts fire on your phone during the grace period to prompt a check-in
  • Automatic SMS alerts - After the grace period, all confirmed contacts receive a text from WellCheck's dedicated number
  • Real text messages - Not from your phone, so contacts take them seriously
  • Custom emergency info - Your emergency notes (medical conditions, location, callback number) are included in every SMS
  • Two-tier alerts - A follow-up SMS is sent 15 minutes later if you're still in emergency mode
  • Works when unconscious - SMS alerts go out automatically, no button to push, no device to wear

How Night Mode Works

  • Activate before bed - Pauses check-in alerts and timers while you sleep
  • Biometric lock - Face ID or Touch ID required to exit — no accidental check-in misses
  • Optional bedtime alarm - Set a time to automatically activate Night Mode each night
  • Optional morning alarm - Resumes check-ins at a set time to start your day
