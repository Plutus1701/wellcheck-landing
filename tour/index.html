Emergency Mode in Action

If you miss a check-in, WellCheck automatically activates emergency calling and sends alerts

Emergency calling demo - tap play if it doesn't start automatically

How Emergency Mode Works

  • Automatic SMS alerts - Your entire call tree gets notified immediately
  • Progressive calling - Large photos show who to call next
  • Simple for kids - Just tap the photo of the person you want to call. Then click the Blue (iOS required) callout.
  • 911 backup - Appears after all contacts have been tried
  • Practice mode - Test the flow without making real calls, tested with kids as young as 3
