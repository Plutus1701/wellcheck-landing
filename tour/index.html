How Check-Ins Work

  • Get reminded - Notification arrives at your scheduled time
  • One tap - Opens the app and confirms you're safe
  • Grace period starts - If you don't respond, you get 5-30 minutes
  • Automatic escalation - After grace period, emergency mode activates

What Happens in an Emergency

  • Immediate SMS alerts - Your customized contact list receives texts from WellCheck's dedicated number
  • Real text messages - Not from your phone, so contacts take them seriously
  • Custom emergency info - Include critical details like medical conditions or locations to check
  • Emergency calling activated - Large photo buttons for calling family members
  • Works when unconscious - No button to push, everything happens automatically

Emergency calling demo - tap play if it doesn't start automatically

Emergency Calling Features

  • Progressive calling - Large photos show who to call next
  • Simple for kids - Just tap the photo, then tap the blue iOS call button
  • Tested with young children - Works with kids as young as 3 years old
  • 911 backup - Appears after all contacts have been tried
  • Practice mode available - Test the flow without making real calls

Why Night Mode Matters

  • For solo parents - What if you have a medical emergency overnight?
  • Kids aren't stuck alone - They can call for help immediately, not hours later when someone notices you haven't checked in
  • Biometric lock - Kids can't accidentally exit the emergency screen
  • Simple interface - Just tap a family member's photo to call them
