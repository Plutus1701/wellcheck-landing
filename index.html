We've learned from every existing solution and built something simple.

✓ Real SMS Messages

Texts come from a dedicated WellCheck number — not your phone. Contacts take them seriously, they will only hear from us if you miss a check-in.

✓ Works When Unconscious

No button to push during a medical emergency. No need to call out to your device. Automatic alerts based on missed check-ins, not requiring you to be conscious or able to reach your device.

Built for Real Emergencies

Flexible Check-In Intervals

Interval mode (every 1–12 hours) or scheduled times (e.g. 9am, 2pm, 9pm daily). Your schedule, your way — not a one-size-fits-all solution.

Smart Grace Periods

Configurable 5-30 minute grace period prevents false alarms when you're just running late or in the shower.

Automated SMS Alerts

Entire app contact list receives texts automatically from WellCheck's number. No phone to unlock, no messages to approve.

Optional Location Sharing

Enable location sharing to include your last known GPS location as a Google Maps link in emergency alerts — so your contacts know exactly where to look for you.

Home Screen Widgets

Check in directly from your home screen — no need to open the app. iOS 17+ supports background check-ins from the widget button.

Night Mode

Pause interval check-in alerts overnight. No accidental missed check-ins while you sleep. Automatically resumes in the morning.

Two Ways to Check In

Set specific times each day. Perfect if you have a routine and want check-ins tied to your actual schedule.
