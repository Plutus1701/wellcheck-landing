SMS Consent Process

How Consent is Collected

WellCheck collects explicit prior consent for SMS alerts through a multi-step verification process:

  1. User adds contact: A WellCheck user manually adds a trusted contact within the app, providing their name, phone number, and email address
  2. Email consent request sent: The contact receives an email explaining the service and requesting consent
  3. Contact reviews and decides: Contact clicks a link to review what they're consenting to and can Accept or Decline
  4. Explicit acceptance required: Only after clicking "Accept" on the consent form will the contact receive SMS alerts
  5. Welcome SMS sent: After accepting, contact receives a welcome SMS with opt-out instructions
Important: Contacts must explicitly accept via email before receiving any SMS alerts. There is no implied or passive consent - clicking "Accept" is required.

Sample Email Consent Request

Subject: [User Name] added you as an emergency contact Hi [Contact Name], [User Full Name] has added you as an emergency contact in WellCheck, a health monitoring app. What you're consenting to: - Receive automated SMS alerts if they miss a check-in - Alerts indicate potential medical emergency - Expected frequency: Rarely (hopefully never) [Review Consent Request Button] Learn more: wellcheckapp.com This consent request will expire in 7 days.

Consent Form (Web-Based)

When contacts click the email link, they see a web form with:

  • Clear explanation of what they're consenting to
  • Two buttons: Accept or Decline
  • Link to WellCheck website for more information
  • Notice that consent expires in 7 days if not responded to

Sample Welcome SMS (Sent After Acceptance Only)

Hi [Contact Name]! [User Full Name] added you as an emergency contact in WellCheck. You'll receive alerts ONLY if [User Full Name] misses a check-in. Learn more: wellcheckapp.com Reply STOP to opt out

Sample Emergency Alert SMS

⚠️ WELLCHECK EMERGENCY ALERT [User Full Name] missed a safety check-in and may need immediate assistance. Scheduled check-in: [Time] on [Date] Grace period ended: [Time] Alert sent: [Current Time] IMPORTANT INFO: [Custom emergency notes if provided] Please verify [User Full Name] is safe. Call immediately: [User Phone Number] This is an automated alert from WellCheck. Verify: wellcheckapp.com/v/[alert-id] Reply STOP to opt out

Opt-Out Process

Recipients can opt out at any time by:
  • Declining the email consent request (before any SMS sent)
  • Replying STOP to any SMS message
  • Replying UNSUBSCRIBE
  • Replying HELP for more information

Opt-outs are processed immediately and permanently. Once opted out, the contact will not receive any further messages.

Message Frequency

Contacts receive SMS messages only when:

  • After accepting email consent: One welcome message
  • During emergencies: When the user misses a check-in (rare)

Expected frequency: Very low - typically zero messages per month unless there's a genuine emergency.

Data Collected for Consent

To manage consent, we store:

  • Contact email address (for consent request)
  • Consent status (pending/confirmed/rejected)
  • Consent timestamp
  • Phone number (only if consent accepted)
  • User's full name (displayed in consent request)

Data retention: Consent records stored for 7 days if no response, permanently if accepted or rejected.

Consent Expiration

Email consent requests expire after 7 days if not responded to. The contact can be re-added and will receive a new consent request.
