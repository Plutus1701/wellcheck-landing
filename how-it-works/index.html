How WellCheck Works

Three actors. Two paths. One goal — make sure someone knows when you need help.

📱 iPhone / Apple Watch
🔥 Firebase
📨 Twilio
Path A

You Check In On Time

🕐
Check-in time arrives Based on your interval or scheduled time. WellCheck has pre-scheduled an emergency alert in Firebase to fire if you don't respond.
↓
🔔
WellCheck notifies you A notification arrives on your iPhone or Apple Watch. You can also start a check-in from Activity Mode on your Watch.
iPhone / Watch
↓
✅
You tap "I'm OK" From the notification, inside the app, or from your Apple Watch. No app open required for the notification action.
iPhone / Watch
↓
🔥
Firebase records the check-in The pending emergency alert is cancelled. A new one is scheduled for the end of your next check-in cycle.
Firebase
↓
🔄
Clock resets — you're on track WellCheck is quiet until your next check-in window. No alerts, no messages. All is well.
Path B

You Miss a Check-In

🕐
Check-in time arrives Same as Path A — WellCheck has a pending emergency alert queued in Firebase, timed for the end of your grace period.
↓
🔔
WellCheck notifies you A notification arrives. You don't respond.
iPhone / Watch
↓
⚠️
Grace period begins Urgent, repeating alerts fire on your phone — every 30 seconds to 2 minutes — for the duration of your grace period (5–30 min, your choice).
iPhone / Watch
↓
Do you check in before the grace period ends?
✓ Yes
You tap "I'm OK" Check-in recorded. Clock resets.
↓
Firebase cancels the alert The scheduled emergency trigger is deleted. No SMS is sent.
↓
✅ All clear Your contacts hear nothing. WellCheck resets to your next check-in window.
✗ No
Grace period ends No check-in recorded. The pre-scheduled trigger fires.
↓
Firebase activates the alert The emergency trigger runs directly from WellCheck's backend — your phone does not need to be on or reachable.
↓
Twilio delivers the SMS One text is sent to every confirmed contact simultaneously, from WellCheck's dedicated number.
↓
🚨 Contacts are notified They receive your name, the missed check-in time, your callback number, and your last known location (if enabled).

What Your Contacts Receive

From: WellCheck Emergency (+1 800-XXX-XXXX)
WELLCHECK ALERT — Sarah Johnson missed their scheduled check-in at 2:00 PM. The grace period ended at 2:15 PM. Please check on them or call (207) 555-0192 immediately.

Last known location: maps.google.com/…

Reply STOP to opt out.

The alert fires from Firebase, not from your phone.
If your device is off, out of battery, or out of signal — the alert still goes out. The moment you checked in, the clock was set. Silence after that is the signal.
