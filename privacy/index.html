WellCheck Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Simple Summary: WellCheck stores all your data locally on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to servers or shared with third parties. Delete the app = delete your data.

What Data WellCheck Uses

All information is stored only on your device:

  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers, and photos of trusted adults you choose
  • Check-In Data: Your check-in times, alarm schedule, and app settings
  • Emergency Alerts: Record of when alerts were sent

Where it's stored: Your iPhone only. Not uploaded anywhere.

Permissions Required

Contacts: To select trusted adults from your existing contacts

Photos (Optional): To choose custom photos for contacts

Notifications: For check-in reminders and emergency alerts

Messages (SMS): To send emergency alerts to your contacts (requires your approval per iOS requirements)

You can manage permissions in: Settings → Privacy → WellCheck

Your Data Rights

  • View: Open WellCheck → Settings
  • Delete contacts: Swipe left on any contact in the app
  • Delete everything: Delete the WellCheck app

Third-Party Services

None. WellCheck only uses Apple's built-in iOS features. No third-party analytics, tracking, or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

WellCheck can be used by children for emergency calling but must be set up by an adult. Since all data stays on your device, no information about children (or anyone) is transmitted or stored externally.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your iPhone's built-in security. Since nothing is transmitted over the network, there's nothing to intercept.

Future Updates

If features like iCloud sync are added in future updates, you'll be asked for explicit permission before any data syncing is enabled.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email: support@wellcheck.app

By using WellCheck, you agree to this privacy policy.
This policy complies with Apple App Store requirements, COPPA, CCPA, and GDPR.
